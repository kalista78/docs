## One Wallet, All Chains

Your AceBot wallet address works on all supported chains. The same address can hold:
- S tokens on Sonic
- MON tokens on Monad
- ETH on Ink
- HYPE on HyperEVM
- XPL on Plasma

<Info>
Each chain requires its own native token for gas fees. Make sure you have native tokens on any chain you want to trade on.
</Info>

## Per-Chain Settings

Some features have chain-specific configurations:

### Default Buy Amount
Set different default buy amounts for each chain:
1. **âš™ï¸ Settings** â†’ **Default Buy Amount**
2. Select a chain
3. Enter your preferred amount

### Rakeback Tiers
Your trading volume tier is tracked separately per chain. High volume on Sonic doesn't affect your Monad tier.

### Referral Rewards
Claim rewards independently for each chain. Rewards are paid in the chain's native token.
