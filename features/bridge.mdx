## Bridge Fees and Costs

When you bridge, you pay:

| Cost | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Bridge Fee** | Fee charged by the bridge protocol |
| **Gas (Source)** | Gas to send tokens from source chain |
| **Gas (Destination)** | Gas to receive tokens on destination chain |

All costs are shown in USD before you confirm.

<Tip>
The MAX preset automatically reserves a small gas buffer (0.1%) to ensure you can pay for gas on the destination chain.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Bridge Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bridge is taking too long" icon="clock">
    Bridge times can extend during network congestion. Check:
    - Source transaction on source chain explorer
    - Wait 10-15 minutes before investigating
    - Contact support if stuck over 30 minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Received less than expected" icon="money-bill-wave">
    Within slippage tolerance is normal. If significantly less:
    - Check the quote slippage you set
    - Bridge routes may change based on liquidity
    - Contact support for major discrepancies
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transaction failed" icon="xmark">
    Common causes:
    - Insufficient balance for gas
    - Slippage exceeded
    - Network congestion

    Your funds remain on the source chain. Try again with adjusted settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tokens not showing on destination" icon="eye-slash">
    - Wait for full confirmation (check explorer)
    - Use `/balance` to force refresh
    - Make sure you're viewing the correct chain
    - Check the transaction on destination explorer
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
