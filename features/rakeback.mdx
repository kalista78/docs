# Rakeback Rewards

Rakeback returns a percentage of your trading fees based on how much you trade. The more you trade, the higher your tier and the more you earn back.

## How Rakeback Works

1. **You trade** on any supported chain
2. **Fees are charged** (1% of trading volume)
3. **Rakeback accrues** based on your tier
4. **You claim** when you're ready

## Rakeback Calculation Example

**Scenario:** You're Silver tier (10%) with referral bonus (+10%) = 20% rakeback

You make a trade:
- Trade volume: 1,000 S
- Platform fee (1%): 10 S
- Your rakeback (20%): 2 S

After the trade, 2 S is added to your pending rakeback.

## Tier Progression

Tiers are based on **lifetime volume**, not recent activity:
- Volume from all trades adds up
- You can only go up in tiers, never down
- Progress carries across sessions

<Tip>
Focus on consistent trading rather than occasional large trades. Volume accumulates over time.
</Tip>

## Rakeback vs Referral Rewards

| Aspect | Rakeback | Referral Rewards |
|--------|----------|------------------|
| **Source** | Your own trading | Others' trading |
| **Rate** | 0-30% (tier-based) | 20-30% (time-based) |
| **Earned from** | Fees you pay | Fees referrals pay |
| **Separate claim** | Yes | Yes |

Both systems work independently and stack with each other.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do rakeback rewards expire?">
    No. Unclaimed rakeback stays in your account until you claim it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose my tier?">
    No. Tiers are based on lifetime volume and can only increase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does volume from failed trades count?">
    No. Only successfully executed trades count toward volume.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is volume tracked per chain or total?">
    Per chain. Each chain has its own tier progression.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
