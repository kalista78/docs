# Start Trading in 5 Minutes

This guide walks you through setting up AceBot and making your first trade. No prior experience needed.

## Before You Begin

You'll need:
- Telegram installed on your device
- Some crypto to deposit (native token for your chosen chain)

## Quick Commands

After setup, you can trade even faster with commands:

```
/buy 0x1234...5678 5       # Buy with 5 native tokens
/sell 0x1234...5678 50%    # Sell 50% of your position
/balance                    # Check your balances
```
