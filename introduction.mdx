## Why Traders Choose AceBot

### Instant Execution
Your trades execute in seconds. Paste a token address, choose your amount, and you're done.

### Multi-Chain Native
Trade on Sonic, Monad, Ink, HyperEVM, and Plasma from a single interface. Switch chains with one tap.

### Advanced Orders
Set limit orders that trigger at your target price. Configure DCA strategies to build positions automatically over time.

### Rewards That Compound
Earn up to 30% rakeback on trading fees based on your volume tier. Refer friends for an additional 10% bonus.

### Bank-Grade Security
Your private keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM and never leave your control. Keys are never displayed in Telegram chat.
