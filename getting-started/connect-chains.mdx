## Enable or Disable Chains

You can hide chains you don't use to simplify your interface.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap **âš™ï¸ Settings** from the main menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Manage Chains">
    Select **Manage Chains**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Toggle Chains">
    - Tap a chain to **enable** it (checkmark appears)
    - Tap again to **disable** it (checkmark removed)

    Disabled chains won't appear in your chain selector or trading menus.

    <Warning>
    You must have at least one chain enabled at all times.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## One Wallet, Multiple Chains

Your AceBot wallet works on all supported chains. The same wallet address can hold:
- S tokens on Sonic
- MON tokens on Monad
- ETH on Ink
- HYPE on HyperEVM
- XPL on Plasma

When you switch chains, your wallet automatically shows the balance for that chain.

<Info>
Each chain has its own native token for gas fees. Make sure you have native tokens on each chain you want to trade on.
</Info>

## Bridge Between Chains

Need to move funds between chains? Use the built-in bridge:

1. Tap **ğŸŒ‰ Bridge** from the main menu
2. Select source chain and destination chain
3. Choose amount (25%, 50%, MAX, or custom)
4. Confirm the bridge transaction

The bridge uses Li.Fi to find the best route for your transfer.

<Tip>
Bridging takes a few minutes depending on the chains involved. You'll receive status updates as the transfer progresses.
</Tip>
